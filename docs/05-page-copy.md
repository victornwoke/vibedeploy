## 5.2 Problem Section

### How It Works Section Headline

Most AI-generated apps are not production-ready. Here's why that matters.

### Card 1

Title: Secrets in Plain Sight -- Description: AI coding tools often commit
environment variables and API keys to public repositories. One scan by a
malicious bot can expose your entire infrastructure.

### Card 2

Title: No Recovery Plan -- Description: When (not if) something breaks, founders
with no backup strategy face complete data loss. Most vibe-coded apps have never
been tested for failure scenarios.

### Card 3

Title: Zero Monitoring -- Description: Your app goes down at 2am on a Saturday.
You find out on Monday from an angry user. Without uptime monitoring and
alerting, you're flying blind.

## 5.4 Services Page Copy

### Section Headline

Four ways to go from risky to production-ready.

### Service 1 -- £99 Production Readiness Audit

What you get: A manual review of your checker results by Victor, a written
traffic-light report covering every risk category, a prioritised fix list, and a
30-minute call to walk through the findings. Delivery: within 48--72 hours of
booking.

### Service 2 -- £500 Production Launch Fix

What you get: Victor fixes your top 3 critical infrastructure issues. Common
fixes include: setting up GitHub Actions CI/CD, moving secrets to environment
variables, adding SSL and domain configuration, creating a basic monitoring
setup. Delivery: 5--7 business days.

### Service 3 -- £1,500+ Full MVP Production Setup

What you get: A complete production infrastructure setup for your app. Includes:
CI/CD pipeline, secrets and environment variable management, staging and
production environment separation, monitoring and alerting, backup and recovery
procedures, deployment documentation. Delivery: 2--4 weeks. Price varies based
on app complexity.

### Service 4 -- £500--£1,000/month DevOps Care Plan

What you get: Victor acts as your on-call DevOps team. Monthly deliverables:
uptime monitoring with alerting, cloud cost review and optimisation, deployment
support, monthly infrastructure report, and priority access for incidents.
Commitment: minimum 3 months.
